Our Story

Established to Solve Real-World Technology Challenges

Sensormind Private Limited was established in 2023 with a vision to leverage innovative technologies to address real-world challenges and create value-driven solutions for individuals, businesses, and industries.

We are committed to delivering reliable, efficient, and technology-enabled services that enhance operational performance, safety, and productivity.

Our work spans IoT-based optimized solutions, consultancy for IoT applications, and skill-based training programs. Prehri, our rolling shutter safety solution, reflects that mission in a practical product for existing shutter infrastructure.
